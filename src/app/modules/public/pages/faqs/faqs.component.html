Open Credential Publisher Learning & Employment Wallet FAQ


GENERAL

What kind of credentials can I import into Open Credential Publisher Learning & Employment Wallet?
Currently you can import any credentials that adhere to an Open Badge or Comprehensive Learner Record format.

What does it mean when a credential is Verifiable?
A verifiable credential means that the issuing organization can programmatically prove they issued the credential to you. This step is similar to a university seal and allows others to believe the veracity of the assertions made by the credential.

Can credentials in Open Credential Publisher Learning & Employment Wallet be seen by anyone else?
No. Credentials are your property and the contents of those credentials cannot be viewed unless explicitly shared by you.

What happens if one of my credentials expires?
The credential will still show up in your Open Credential Publisher Learning & Employment Wallet but will not be able to be verified and will show an expiration date.
